Skills and Experience

Web Development

Whether you need a new website, or updates to an existing application. if it involves the web, I can certainly help.

I write code that conforms to the W3C's web standards and semantic best practices. I use technologies like HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and AngularJS, to transform static artwork into mobile first, pixel prefect, interactive and fully functional interfaces.

Responsive Design

Smartphones, tablets, even your television has internet access. Your site should be as adaptable as your customers.

How do you keep a brand consistent, while accommodating for various existing and emerging devices? I build using a responsive, mobile first grid systems, built on frameworks such as Bootstrap to ensure smarter, more future-proof sites.

Source Control

It's very easy to start building without considering consistency, or future development.

I'm familiar with the principles of source control and strive to write clean, maintainable code. I use tools like Git and services like GitHub to ensure consistency in my code, to conform to best practices and to keep coding. Wether alongside a team, or remotely.

Backend Development

While the visual side of modern websites drive your customers, the backend drives your application performance.

I know what happens under the hood of your Rails applications. From creating Models & Controllers, all the way through to routing, caching and setting up AJAX requests. I only use the most mature gems to ensure your app are production ready.

I'm Always Learning

The world of development constantly changes, so it's important to stay humble, and keep learning.

Development branches out in many complex and interesting directions. Front End, Back End, Full Stack, Mobile, even VR. I am more than willing to learn, and always open to new tools and techniques.

Project Management

Code is what brings an idea from sketch on a notepad, to a living website. But it isn't the only thing.

Budgeting, deadlines, co-ordination, organisation. Words many of us aren't fond of. Nevertheless, they play a critical role in successful projects. I've worked for startups, and for corporations. I know how to get to the finish line.
